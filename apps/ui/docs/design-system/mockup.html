Why Remote Falcon

Everything you need to run an interactive show.

From the first vote to the final song, give your viewers a reason to come back every night.

♪

Jukebox & Voting modes

Two interactive flavors out of the box. Switch in a click — or schedule them by night.

  • Real-time queue with rate-limit safeguards
  • Pre-show voting windows with live tallies
  • Per-sequence cooldowns and visibility rules
▥

A viewer page you actually own

Drop in a template, or write your own HTML/CSS. The editor lints as you type and previews live.

  • Curated, mobile-first templates
  • Inline validation with the new Monaco editor
  • Custom domain & SSL included
◉

Find & be found on the Shows Map

Opt in to the global directory and let viewers discover shows in their neighborhood — with live status.

  • Auto-clustered by region with live "playing now"
  • Filter by features (jukebox, voting, FM)
  • Embed a regional map on your own site
